Why It Changed

Playbook
How to score 25/25
S1 — 3/3Call Takeover
Confirm name + key info in one sentence, then ask your first discovery question within 30 seconds. Don't chat. Get in and go.
S2 — 4/4Building Your Case
Ask carrier, price, why shopping, rent/own, all drivers, toys, tickets. Find the real reason. Every answer becomes ammunition for your close.
S3 — 4/4Presenting the Quote
Use their words: "You're paying $210 for liability-only on a 2024 Accord. I've got you at $185 with full coverage. One accident and that car is gone — now it's protected."
Playbook
The money sections
S4 — 7/7Objection Handling
Preframe with discovery: "Your rate jumped $40 last renewal. For less than what you're paying now, you get better coverage and it takes 10 minutes." When they push back, use THEIR situation. Then close again.
S5 — 7/7Closing Throughout
Start with: "If I can save you money with better coverage, is that something you'd want to start today?" Trial close during discovery. Close after quoting. Close after objections. At least 3 asks. The close isn't a moment — it's the whole call.
