This Week's Focus
FOCUS 01
Ask the two questions you're skipping
Your S2 avg is 2.1 — the lowest section. On most calls you're missing "What are you paying now?" and "Why are you shopping?" These two questions unlock everything downstream. Without them, you can't compare old vs new (S3) or preframe objections (S4). Ask them on every single call.
FOCUS 02
Use their words to handle objections
Your S4 avg is 3.0 when objections come up — you're responding but not preframing. On the Iliana Ibarra call you used her situation to address concerns before she raised them. That's S4 level 4 (5 pts). Do that by using what they tell you in discovery: their current price, their coverage gaps, their reason for shopping.
FOCUS 03
Start closing earlier
S5 avg is 2.6 — you're waiting until the end to ask. Start every call with: "If I can save you money with better coverage, is that something you'd want to start today?" Then ask again after quoting. Again after handling objections. 3+ asks across the call = S5 level 5 (7 pts under new rubric).
